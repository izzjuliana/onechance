One Chance

a website dedicated to informing and stopping the violence Honduran women face in their lives.

What is going on?

A widespread problem in central america, specifically in Honduras, is the violence women face in their day-to-day lives. Sadly, this country has one of the highest rates of femicide which is why it is so important to shed light on this issue. More people need to know and if able, to donate to organizations that support this cause, so that these women can live in a safer world.
